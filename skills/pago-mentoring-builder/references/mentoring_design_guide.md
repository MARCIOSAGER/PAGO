## 1. Two-Day Intensive Structure

The P.A.G.O. mentoring follows a **Diagnosis → Reconstruction** arc:

| Day | Theme | Emotional Arc | Objective |
|-----|-------|--------------|-----------|
| Day 1 | Diagnóstico | Acolhimento → Vulnerabilidade → Clareza | See reality honestly |
| Day 2 | Reconstrução | Esperança → Ação → Compromisso | Build with foundation |

**Time blocks:** 08:00-16:00 with 15-min coffee breaks (AM/PM) and 60-min lunch.

**Balance rule:** ~40% teaching, ~40% exercises, ~20% group sharing/prayer.

## 2. Day 1 Design Principles

Day 1 breaks the person down to honest self-assessment. The tone is **safe, welcoming, non-judgmental**.

**Block sequence:**
1. Photography of current life (P.A.G.O. self-assessment 0-10)
2. Life wheel diagnosis (10 areas scored)
3. Naming the pain (open wounds, unprocessed grief, avoided feelings)
4. Identifying repetitive cycles (self-sabotage patterns, automatic reactions)
5. Current state narrative (free writing — most important exercise)

**Key dynamic:** "A Caixa das Emoções" — anonymous written emotions placed in a box, prayed over collectively.

**Facilitator warning:** Day 1 is emotionally intense. Prepare for tears, silence, resistance. Never rush processing.

## 3. Day 2 Design Principles

Day 2 rebuilds with practical tools and commitments. The tone is **hopeful, practical, committed**.

**Block sequence:**
1. Reflection on Day 1 insights
2. Personal Principles Charter (5 non-negotiable biblical principles)
3. Alignment Map (vertical/horizontal/internal)
4. Government Audit (4 domains) + Secret Routine design
5. P.A.G.O. Action Map + Pending Obedience Inventory
6. Life Declaration P.A.G.O. (written and signed)
7. Ceremony: voluntary reading of declarations

**Closing:** Collective signing of the P.A.G.O. Declaration + prayer of commissioning.

## 4. Weekly Follow-up Structure (8 Weeks)

Each session: 60-90 minutes.

| Phase | Duration | Activity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Opening | 10 min | Prayer + one-word mood check-in |
| P.A.G.O. Check-in | 20 min | Score 4 pillars + share 1 win |
| Deep Share | 15 min | 1 person shares a weekly challenge |
| Brief Teaching | 15 min | Deep-dive into one pillar |
| Weekly Action | 10 min | Each declares 1 concrete action |
| Closing Prayer | 10 min | Mutual intercession |

**Weekly themes:** Wk1-Consolidation, Wk2-Princípio, Wk3-Alinhamento Vertical, Wk4-Alinhamento Horizontal, Wk5-Governo Espiritual/Emocional, Wk6-Governo Financeiro, Wk7-Obediência Radical, Wk8-Integration/Legacy.

## 5. Exercise Design Patterns

**Self-assessment exercises:** Always use 0-10 scale + "why this score?" open text.

**Deep writing exercises:** Provide prompting questions, not blank pages. Use italicized guiding questions before each writing space.

**Group sharing:** Trios for safety, full group only for volunteers.

**Action exercises:** Always include "what will I measure?" and "by when?" columns.

**Declaration exercises:** Written in first person, signed, dated, witnessed.

## 6. Facilitation Principles

1. Facilitate — don't fix. God transforms; you guide.
2. Create safety before requesting vulnerability.
3. Don't judge, minimize, or rush.
4. Respect silence and tears.
5. Your own P.A.G.O. journey is your greatest leadership tool.
6. Pray before every session.

## 7. Critical Situation Handling

| Situation | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Intense crying | Stay silent nearby. Offer water. Pray silently. "No rush here." |
| Severe trauma | Don't counsel clinically. Empathize. Refer to professional support privately. |
| Method resistance | "That's okay. What matters is your honesty with yourself." |
| Heavy group energy | Pause 5 min. Brief prayer. Short worship song to shift atmosphere. |
| Dominant speaker | "What you bring is important. Let's save it for a one-on-one. I'd like to hear others too." |
